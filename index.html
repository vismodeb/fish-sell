FAQ’s – Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping live fish is a lot more common and less stressful than you may realize. Fish are shipped overnight directly to your door. Fish and water are placed in extra thick plastic shipping bags. The shipping bags are filled with pure oxygen and sealed securely with rubber bands. The bag is then placed inside a heavy duty cardboard shipping container and sealed. During the warmer months an ice pack is placed under the bag in order to help keep the fish/water cool during transit. Orders are packaged later in the day to minimize the amount of time the fish will be in the shipping container. Most packages are delivered within 24 Hours. Deaths during shipping are rare.
